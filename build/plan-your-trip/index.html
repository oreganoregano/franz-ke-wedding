Venue

We've selected the Vinoy in Tampa, FL for our wedding. Our ceremony will take place in the romantic tea garden. Our reception will take place in the Grand Ballroom.

Things to Do

  • St. Petersburg Museum of Fine Arts
  • Dali Museum
  • Chihuly Collection
  • Take a walk through North Straub Park
  • Grab some gelato at Paciugo
  • Enjoy rooftop cocktails at the Canopy (at the Birchwood Hotel.)
  • Relax at the spa at the Vinoy
  • St. Pete Beach is just a quick 20 minute drive away
